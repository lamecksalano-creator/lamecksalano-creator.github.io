Welcome

I'm Lameck Salano, a sales and marketing professional based in Nairobi, Kenya, with hands-on experience gained at Samsung Ltd. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Business and am currently completing a Diploma in Travel and Tourism Management at Mount Kenya University, expected August 2026.

This site is where I share my professional background and write about the topics I know best: sales strategy, marketing, and Kenya's tourism and wildlife spaces.
